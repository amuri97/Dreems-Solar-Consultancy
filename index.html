About Us

Our mission is to install affordable Roof Top Solar Power Packs of 1KW and 2KW in every house, and Wind Power Packs in every village and urban house. This reduces the load on the POWER GRID, which is dependent on government power utilities. We are dedicated to creating awareness about energy conservation among consumers and promoting sustainable practices.

We also aim to replace existing street lights, including 4-40W Fluorescent lamps, H.P.M.V, and H.P.S.P lamps, with Solid State Luminaries (LED lights). This transition significantly reduces energy consumption and helps contribute to a greener future.
