About Us

Dyumani Renewable Energy Efficient Management Systems (DREEMS) is a renewable energy consulting firm set up by eminent and experienced energy-sector veterans and professionals. We specialize in Solar PV project consultation and rooftop installation, having undertaken multiple projects across the state of Andhra Pradesh.

M Devendranath Reddy

Founder and Working Partner of DREEMS, is an Energy-sector professional with over 45 years of experience across several verticals. He has consulted for several solar PV projects ranging from MW size to solar rooftop PV projects, bringing a wealth of expertise in renewable energy and energy sectors.
